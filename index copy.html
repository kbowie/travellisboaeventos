The foundation of this experience is a meticulously planned, carefully thought-out real tour, transformed by a team of professionals into an immersive theatrical performance.

With humor, ease, and authenticity, the resurrected King José will share spicy stories about the lives of aristocrats and commoners. You'll feel like an active participant in this theatrical spectacle—a medieval fairytale brought to life through headphones. Detailed Route Overview

We’ll begin our journey in a square haunted by the restless souls of beautiful young women executed for witchcraft. Discover who watches over Lisbon and who gifted Brazil its independence.

José will guide you to the finest examples of an architectural style born in Portugal during the Age of Discovery—magnificent facades adorned with stone lacework that will transport you centuries back in time.

Our ghostly guide will unveil the secret of a popular Portuguese dessert, still guarded by monks away from prying eyes. He’ll show you a remarkable spot that perfectly illustrates the influence of the Arab world on local culture.

Over a glass of cherry liqueur, he’ll share insights into the lives and traditions of modern Lisboetas.
You’ll step into a mysterious church with pink marble walls, scarred by the devastating power of an ancient fire, leaving an indelible impression. Learn the tale of a mysterious queen crowned posthumously, whose lifeless hand was kissed by her subjects in a gesture of reverence.

Add a touch of heroic battles, depicted on stunning blue azulejo tiles, a stroll through the medieval streets of one of the city’s oldest neighborhoods, and a sprinkle of local legends. Along the way, you’ll receive detailed descriptions of local dishes and tips on which wines to order in our taverns.

Have you heard of fado? Did you know that this iconic Portuguese music was once sung by the homeless, rogues, and women of ill repute? Would you like to listen?

During the tour, we’ll ascend to the base of the castle, visit the best viewpoints, savor local delicacies, and receive recommendations on the perfect souvenirs to bring home. Learn the story behind the “black rooster” from souvenir shops.

We’ll conclude our journey at one of Europe’s most beautiful squares, where José will bid you farewell on the banks of the Tagus River.

There’s so much to say about Lisbon and Portugal as a whole, but José has handpicked the most intriguing and fascinating moments to share with you during this two-hour tour.
